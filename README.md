# Revenue Forecast Integrity Audit
### Pipeline Quality & CRM Performance Analysis | Global B2B Tech Firm

---

## Business Problem

A global B2B tech firm had a $22.7M revenue pipeline - but was consistently missing revenue targets. The pipeline looked healthy on the surface. The underlying reality was different.

The diagnosis: **a forecast gap crisis** - where stale pipeline inflation, dangerous concentration risk, and regional underperformance were generating false revenue forecasts that were driving wrong business decisions at the executive level.

---

## Context

**Domain:** Revenue Operations / CRM Analytics / Forecasting  
**Tools:** Microsoft Excel, Tableau. 
**Scope:** Full pipeline quality audit - segmentation, rep analysis, regional performance, forecast accuracy  
**Period:** March 2025 - March 2026  
**Relevance to Payments/Fintech:** Directly mirrors merchant revenue forecasting, CRM pipeline quality management, and revenue integrity functions in payments and fintech operations

## Key Findings

### Executive Performance

| Metric | Finding | Benchmark | Status |
|--------|---------|-----------|--------|
| Total Pipeline | $22.7M | — | — |
| Active Sales | 20% of total revenue | 60%+ | 🔴 Critical |
| Win Rate | 22.71% | 35-45% | 🔴 Critical |

### The "Big Three" Concentration Problem

**Single-Rep Concentration Risk:**
- One rep (Jeffrey Johnson) generating **47% of total pipeline**
- This is not a performance achievement — it is a **business continuity risk**
- If this rep leaves, nearly half the pipeline disappears overnight

**Revenue Concentration:**
- Top 3 accounts = **67% of total revenue**
- Extreme dependency on a small number of relationships

**Industry Concentration:**
- Healthcare is the leading industry, generating over 21x more revenue than the bottom - performing sector (Manufacturing)
- Manufacturing sector disproportionately represented, with over 75% loss rate. The highest in the company
- Sector downturn = significant revenue exposure

### Forecast Gap Root Causes

**Root Cause 1 - Stale Pipeline Inflation**
Deals sitting in the pipeline long past realistic close dates - artificially inflating the headline pipeline number. The $22.7M figure was not $22.7M of real opportunity.

**Root Cause 2 - Weak Win Rate (22%)**
Nearly 4 in 5 pipeline deals were not converting. Either qualification standards were too loose, or deals were being moved to later stages prematurely.

**Root Cause 3 - Regional Underperformance**
South region significantly underperforming relative to other markets - dragging overall conversion metrics.

**Root Cause 4 - No Pipeline Hygiene Process**
No systematic process for removing or re-qualifying stale deals - allowing false optimism to compound over time.

### The Core Insight

> *"A pipeline number is only as valuable as the quality of the deals inside it. When stale deals inflate your forecast, every business decision downstream - hiring, budgeting, capacity planning - is built on a false foundation."*

---

## Recommendations

### 1. Immediate Pipeline Hygiene
Remove or re-qualify all deals that have not had meaningful activity in 60+ days. This will reduce the headline pipeline number - but replace a false number with a true one.

**Business Impact:** More accurate revenue forecast. Better resource allocation. Reduced end-of-quarter surprise.

### 2. Rep Risk Redistribution
Actively redistribute pipeline away from the single high-concentration rep. Assign accounts to additional reps with structured handover.

**Business Impact:** Eliminates single-point-of-failure risk. More resilient pipeline structure.

### 3. Qualification Gate Enforcement
Implement mandatory qualification criteria before deals advance past stage 2. Train all reps on consistent qualification standards.

**Business Impact:** Higher win rates on smaller, cleaner pipeline. Improved forecast accuracy.

### 4. Regional Performance Intervention
Deploy targeted coaching and resource support to South region. Identify whether underperformance is a market, process, or capability issue.

**Business Impact:** Recovery of underperforming corridor revenue.

### 5. Forecast Integrity Dashboard
Build a real-time dashboard that separates:
- Active pipeline (recent activity within 30 days)
- At-risk pipeline (no activity 30-60 days)
- Stale pipeline (no activity 60+ days)

**Business Impact:** Leadership sees the true pipeline picture at all times.
